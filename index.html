<p>I am currently a Senior Research Associate working with <a href="https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~cm542/"><u>Prof. Cecilia Mascolo</u></a> and an Affliated Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Cambridge. Prior to that I was a postdoctoral researcher working with <a href="https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk/~zhenjili/"><u>Prof. Zhenjiang Li</u></a> at City University of Hong Kong (from Oct 2020 to Dec 2021). I received the B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Shaanxi, China in 2016 and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science advised by <a href="https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk/~zhenjili/"><u>Prof. Zhenjiang Li</u></a> from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China in 2020.</p>

<br>

<p><b>My research focuses on developing intelligent mobile and wearable technologies for human-centered sensing and computing. Currently, I am exploring earable sensing technologies to monitor human wellness, including vital signs monitoring, fitness tracking, and promoting healthy lifestyles through ear-worn sensors. Additionally, I am investigating mobile and wearable technologies for understanding human behaviors, enhancing interactions between humans and their surroundings, as well as identifying and addressing potential privacy leakage issues during these interactions. Overall, my research advances cutting-edge fields such as the Internet of Things (IoT), Human-Computer Interaction, Human-Ambient Interaction, mobile health, and cybersecurity.</b></p>
